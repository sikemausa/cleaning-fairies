Pre / Post Construction Services

Home Owners

No matter what renovations you are completing at your home, you are likely going to require some professional help with the clean up process. Construction dust has a way of getting in your vents and then settles onto all vertical & horizontal surfaces throughout your home or office. Even if you are just doing construction in one area of your home, we suggest cleaning all surrounding areas. We also recommend after you finish construction, getting your ducts professionally cleaned prior to scheduling our crew. This lessens the likelihood of dust re-settling after we complete our job!

Contractors

We work with several construction companies & independent contractors in Boulder country doing post construction clean ups after your job is completed. Our staff understands what it takes to make a home move in ready after a new build or renovation is finalized. We have several references that can be provided upon request. We thrive on business to business relationships and offer discounted rates for bulk work we are contracted to complete post-construction.

CLEAN TIMES PER ROOM DEPENDING ON BUILD UP

  • Kitchens

    Kitchens take 1.5 - 3 hours to clean

    • Stove tops, countertops, sides & edges wiped down
    • Cabinets (insides & outsides) cleaned
    • The exterior of all appliances wiped & polished (fridge, freezer, dishwasher oven)
    • All walls, outlet covers, baseboards, backsplash wipe down
    • All window sills, tracks & accessible glass cleaned
    • Trim around doors & windows wiped down
    • Fronts & bevels of any doors
    • Blinds cleaned
    • Pantries/shelving wiped down
    • Any accessible heating vents wiped down
    • Floors vacuumed & mopped
  • Bathrooms

    Bathrooms take 15 - 75 minutes to clean

    • All surfaces sanitized (sink, countertops, backsplash etc.)
    • Mirrors polished & left streak free
    • Toilet top, seat & base cleaned
    • Shower/Tub wiped out
    • All walls, outlet covers, baseboards & trim wipe down
    • All window sills, tracks & accessible glass cleaned
    • Fronts & bevels of any doors
    • Any built-in shelving wiped down
    • Blinds cleaned
    • Any accessible heating vents wiped down
    • Floors vacuumed & hand moped
  • Living / Dining rooms

    Living rooms & dining rooms take 15 - 45 minutes to clean

    • All walls, outlet covers, baseboards & trim wipe down
    • All window sills, tracks & accessible glass cleaned
    • Fronts & bevels of any doors
    • Any built-in shelving wiped down
    • Ceiling fans cleaned
    • Blinds cleaned
    • Any accessible heating vents wiped down
    • Floors vacuumed & mopped
  • Bedrooms / Offices

    Bedrooms & offfices take 15 - 45 minutes to clean

    • All walls, outlet covers, baseboards & trim wipe down
    • All window sills, tracks & accessible glass cleaned
    • Fronts of any doors
    • Any built-in shelving wiped down
    • Ceiling fans cleaned
    • Blinds cleaned
    • Any accessible heating vents wiped down
    • Floors vacuumed & mopped
  • Stairways / Landings / Hallways

    Stairways, landings, & hallways take 10 - 30 minutes to clean

    • All walls, outlet covers, baseboards & trim wipe down
    • All window sills, tracks & accessible glass cleaned
    • Fronts & bevels of any doors
    • Any built-in shelving wiped down
    • Blinds cleaned
    • Any accessible heating vents wiped down
    • Railings sanitized
    • Floors vacuumed & mopped
