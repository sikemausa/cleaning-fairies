Residential Services

Scheduling Is Easy!

Whether you are signing up for a one time clean or recurring services, we prefer to do a complimentary in home site visit. From there we build a customized estimate that we refer to as a “Spec Sheet”. This information will go over each area of your home and explain what needs to be cleaned and how long the area is estimated to take. Along with your preferences, this spec sheet will be retained to simplify the scheduling of recurring or future cleans!

Call In Cleans vs. Recurring Cleans

Recurring Cleans:

  • We offer several different recurring frequencies: weekly, every other week, every four weeks or every 8 weeks.
  • We make it a priority to get you with the same fairy, on the same day of the week each time to ensure consistent results!
  • You also get priority over any call in clients, if you need to reschedule your clean for any reason.
  • Clients get an hourly price break when signing up for recurring services.
  • Initial cleans will often take a little longer than subsequent cleans, first, because it takes our staff a clean or two to get accustomed to your home, but second, because there will be less buildup on subsequent cleans.

    • Call in Cleans:

  • We save all our “call in clients” information on file to simplify the process of scheduling.
  • You can reach out for special occasions or whenever you are in need of a clean and we will get you scheduled in the next available opening.
  • You can request deep cleans or general cleans depending on your needs!

    • CLEAN TIMES PER ROOM DEPENDING ON BUILD UP

    • Bathroom

      Bathrooms Take 15-60 Minutes to Clean

      General Clean:

      • Sanitize sink, counter tops & any items on them
      • Clean mirrors, leave streak free
      • Wipe down sink handles, toilet paper handles & towel rack
      • Clean toilet, inside, top & base
      • Tidy & straighten where necessary
      • Clean shower/bath walls/floor/door etc.
      • Spot check accessible baseboards, outlet covers & door trim
      • Spot check cabinet fronts
      • Floors vacuumed & mopped
      • Any window sills wiped down
      • Remove any trash

      Deeper Level Items (done by request or on initial clean):

      • Hand wipe all baseboards, doors & trim
      • Clean walls
      • Window tracks opened up and cleaned
      • Full wipe down of cabinet fronts and/or cabinet interiors
      • Accessible heating registers or fans wiped down
    • Bedrooms / Offices

      Bedrooms & Offices Take 10-30 Minutes to Clean

      General Clean:

      • Wet wipe all surfaces (desks, tables, shelving, window sills, headboards etc.)
      • Spot check accessible light fixtures & lamp bases
      • Dry dust any TV or computer equipment
      • Spot check accessible baseboards & outlet covers
      • Tidy where necessary
      • Make bed if sheets are left out, straighten bed if not
      • Check corners for cobwebs
      • Vacuum and/or mop floors
      • Spot check ceiling fan, if accessible
      • Spot check interior glass, if accessible
      • Remove any garbage

      Deeper Level Items (done by request or on initial clean):

      • Accessible heating registers wiped down
      • Doors & trim around doors & windows cleaned
      • Walls cleaned
      • Vacuum bed or couches
      • Full baseboard clean
      • Window tracks cleaned
      • Blinds cleaned (dry dusted or wet wiped)
      • Items removed from shelving areas & wiped down
    • Kitchens

      Kitchens Take 30-90 Minutes to Clean

      General Clean:

      • Counters, backsplash, sink, stove top all thoroughly wiped down. Items moved as needed.
      • All countertop appliances wiped down (coffee maker, toaster, blender etc.)
      • Spot check fronts of all cabinets (especially around the handles)
      • Polish all large appliances (oven/stove, fridge/freezer, microwave dishwasher)
      • Clean inside microwave
      • Window sills dusted
      • Interior glass spot checked (exterior on glass doors as well)
      • Spot check accessible baseboards & outlet covers
      • Remove any garbage & wipe trash can down

      Deeper Level Items (done by request or on initial clean):

      • Accessible heating registers wiped down
      • Doors & trim around doors & windows cleaned
      • Walls cleaned
      • Full baseboard clean
      • Blinds cleaned (dry dusted or wet wiped)
      • Fronts or tops of cabinets cleaned (top to bottom)
      • Clean insides of any large appliances (fridge, freezer, oven)
    • Living / Dining Rooms

      Living Rooms / Dining Rooms Take 10-30 Minutes to Clean

      General Clean:

      • Wet wipe all surfaces (desks, tables, chairs, shelving, window sills, light fixtures etc.)
      • Spot check accessible light fixtures & lamp bases
      • Dry dust any TV or computer equipment
      • Spot check accessible baseboards & outlet covers
      • Tidy where necessary (fold blankets, straighten toys etc.)
      • Check corners for cobwebs
      • Vacuum and/or mop floors
      • Spot check ceiling fan, if accessible
      • Spot check interior glass, if accessible
      • Get under & around any plants & plant debris

      Deeper Level Items (done by request or on initial clean):

      • Accessible heating registers wiped down
      • Doors & trim around doors & windows cleaned
      • Walls cleaned
      • Vacuum couches
      • Full baseboard clean
      • Window tracks cleaned
      • Blinds cleaned (dry dusted or wet wiped)
      • Items removed from shelving areas & wiped down
    • Stairways / Hallways

      Stairways / Landings / Hallways Take 10-20 Minutes to Clean

      General Clean:

      • Wet wipe all surfaces (tables, shelving, window sills etc.)
      • Spot check accessible light fixtures & lamp bases
      • Sanitize any railings/banisters
      • Dry dust artwork & picture frames
      • Vacuum and/or mop floors
      • Spot check accessible baseboards & outlet covers

      Deeper Level Items (done by request or on initial clean):

      • Accessible heating registers wiped down
      • Doors & trim around doors & windows cleaned
      • Walls cleaned
      • Full baseboard clean
