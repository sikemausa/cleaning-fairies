Move Outs / Move Ins

Make Your Move in / Move out a Breeze!

Move Out & Move In cleans are typically scheduled for roughly twice as long as residential cleans. They not only include all the deeper level items we do in residential, but everything else you would never think to clean! If you moved into a new space that wasn’t as clean as you were expecting, or are moving away and just don’t have time to clean yourself, give us a call. We know that moving is one of humankind’s least favorite endeavors, so let the professionals help you get your deposit back!

Student Housing

SKO Buffs!!!! Because we are located in the heart of this beautiful college town, we specialize in Move Out / Move In cleans for individual students as well as entire student housing complexes. This is a great way to get your security deposit back as well as take the edge off of moving at the end of a long semester.

Entire Buildings

We work with property managers to turn over entire apartment buildings in a day or two if necessary! We get the property ready for the next tenant, working alongside the painters and maintenance folks turning over thousands of properties a year. We also offer property managers bulk rates.

Real Estate

Whether it is cleaning a furnished home for a showing, cleaning an empty home for a closing, or cleaning a home once a realtor's clients have already moved in. If it is in the contract that the home must be professionally cleaned, we provide the realtor or client an invoice and spec sheet to bring to the closing. We have a handful of references from realtors we have been working with for years, because no one wants to move into a dirty home!

CLEAN TIMES PER ROOM DEPENDING ON BUILD UP

  • Kitchen

    Kitchens take 1.5 - 5 hours to clean

    • Stove top & oven taken apart and cleaned
    • Countertops, sides & edges wiped down
    • Cabinets (insides & outsides) cleaned
    • Top, inside & outside of fridge/freezer
    • Dishwasher outside & inside door wiped down
    • All walls, outlet covers, baseboards, back splash wiped down
    • All window sills, tracks & accessible interior glass cleaned
    • Trim around doors & windows wiped down
    • Fronts of any doors spot checked
    • Blinds cleaned
    • Pantries/shelving wiped down
    • Any accessible heating vents wiped down
    • Floors vacuumed & mopped
  • Living / Dining Rooms

    Living rooms and dining rooms take 15 - 45 minutes to clean

    • All walls, outlet covers, baseboards & trim wipeD down
    • All window sills, tracks & accessible interior glass cleaned
    • Fronts of any doors spot checked
    • Any built-in shelving or closets wiped down
    • Ceiling fans cleaned
    • Blinds cleaned
    • Any accessible heating vents wiped down
    • Floors vacuumed & mopped
  • Bedrooms / Offices

    Bedrooms & offices take 15 - 45 minutes to clean

    • All walls, outlet covers, baseboards & trim wiped down
    • All window sills, tracks & accessible interior glass cleaned
    • Fronts of any doors spot checked
    • Any built-in shelving or closets wiped down
    • Ceiling fans cleaned
    • Blinds cleaned
    • Any accessible heating vents wiped down
    • Floors vacuumed & mopped
  • Stairways / Landings / Hallways

    Stairways, landings & hallways take 10 - 30 minutes to clean

    • All walls, outlet covers, baseboards & trim wiped down
    • All window sills, tracks & accessible interior glass cleaned
    • Fronts of any doors
    • Any built-in shelving and closets wiped down
    • Blinds cleaned
    • Any accessible heating vents wiped down
    • Railings sanitized
    • Floors vacuumed & mopped
  • Bathrooms

    Bathrooms take 15 - 75 minutes to clean

    • All surfaces sanitized (sink, countertops, backsplash etc.)
    • Mirrors polished & left streak free
    • Toilet top, seat & base cleaned
    • Shower/Tub deep cleaned with a grout brush
    • All walls, outlet covers, baseboards & trim wiped down
    • All window sills, tracks & accessible interior glass cleaned
    • Fronts of any doors spot checked
    • Any built-in shelving wiped down
    • Blinds cleaned
    • Any accessible heating vents wiped down
    • Floors vacuumed & hand mopped
