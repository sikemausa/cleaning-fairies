About Us

Proud to call Boulder our home

My name is Molly and I am the owner and founder of Boulder’s Cleaning Fairies. In 1996, my daughter Kyla and I moved from Telluride to Boulder, Colorado, and I found myself looking for a job that would enable me to perform my duties as a single parent. Cleaning houses gave me the flexible schedule that I had been looking for! After a couple years of working solo, I had expanded from a one-woman show to an established business with three employees and about 50 clients. The Cleaning Fairies, as we now know it, were born when a client of mine canceled and I decided to clean a neighbor’s house instead as a surprise gift. The client came home and exclaimed, “The cleaning fairy came!” The name stuck and has represented the company ever since! While the company has gone through several changes throughout the years, we have always been proud to call Boulder our home. The fairies use all green cleaning products that we supply, more details about our supplies are listed on a separate tab under the ABOUT US page. We currently service Boulder, Louisville, Lafayette, Gunbarrel, Superior, Niwot, Longmont, Lyons and most surrounding areas. We hope to continue to serve this community for years to come!

Our fairies in the field

We have a crew of 15-20 fairies year round. We are a small business who thrives on the relationships our fairies build with their clients. We consider our fairies like our family, some team members have been with us for almost 10 years! Our fairies are the foundation of this company and are the driver behind our success. We value each and every one of them!

Upon being hired, our fairies are background checked and insured to guarantee a safe outcome every clean. Our training is extensive and takes anywhere from 2-4 weeks depending on level of experience. We can provide liability insurance upon request via email.

A few things to know before you apply

  • Please be aware that our office is located in Central Boulder and the bulk of our customers are in Boulder County.
  • You are expected to have your own reliable vehicle for transportation between accounts.
  • You are required to have a smartphone with access to our calendar sharing application.
  • Some level of cleaning experience is preferred, but not required.
